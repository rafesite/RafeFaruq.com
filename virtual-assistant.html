How do I get started? ▾
Simple — book a free 30-minute discovery call via the button above. We'll discuss your needs and I'll tell you exactly how I can help. No pressure, no commitment.
What are your working hours? ▾
I work Sunday–Thursday, 9AM–6PM Bangladesh time (BST, UTC+6). For urgent tasks I'm reachable async via Slack or email outside those hours.
How do we communicate day-to-day? ▾
Primarily through Slack or email — whichever you prefer. I send daily task updates and we have a weekly check-in call to stay aligned.
What tools do you work with? ▾
I'm comfortable with HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Slack, Google Workspace, Canva, Trello, Asana, and most common VA tools. If you use something else, I adapt fast.

I accept ACH bank transfer (US) and Payoneer. Invoices are sent at the start of each month. Payment is due within 5 business days.
Can I cancel or change my plan? ▾
Yes — you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No lock-in contracts.
Do you sign NDAs? ▾
Absolutely. Confidentiality is non-negotiable. I'm happy to sign your NDA before we begin.
