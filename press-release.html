FAQ

Have questions?
I have answers!

How long does it take to publish? +
Most outlets publish within 3–5 business days after submission. Some premium outlets like Yahoo Finance may take 5–7 days.
Can I choose multiple outlets? +
Yes — you can order multiple packages. Fill out a separate order form for each outlet, or contact me to bundle them.
Do I need to write the article myself? +
No — you can choose our writing service (+$50) and we'll craft a 500-word SEO-optimized press release using your keywords and links.
Will I get proof of publication? +
Yes — you'll receive a full report with live URLs, screenshots, and backlink data for every outlet your story is published on.
What topics are accepted? +
Most business, product, service, and brand topics are accepted. Adult content, gambling, and certain regulated industries may be restricted depending on the outlet.
How do I pay? +
After submitting your inquiry, I'll send you an invoice via ACH bank transfer or Payoneer. Payment is required before submission.
