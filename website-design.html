FAQ

Have questions?
I have answers!

What do I need to provide to get started? +
Just tell me your goals, the pages you need, and share any brand assets (logo, colors, fonts). I'll handle the rest.
Do you use templates or build from scratch? +
Everything is built from scratch — custom designed for your brand. No generic templates.
What if I don't like the design? +
For Landing Pages and Business Websites, you only pay if you're happy with the result. If it's not right, you walk away — no charge, no questions.
Will my website work on mobile? +
Yes — every site I build is fully responsive and tested across devices and screen sizes.
Do you provide hosting? +
I can guide you to the right hosting solution and handle the deployment. Hosting cost is separate and depends on your provider.
How do payments work for E-commerce and Web Apps? +
50% upfront to begin, 50% upon project completion. Payment via ACH bank transfer or Payoneer.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Yes — send me your current site and tell me what you want improved. I'll give you a custom quote based on the scope.
