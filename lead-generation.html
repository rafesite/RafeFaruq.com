Have questions?
I have answers!

Everything you need to know before we start working together. Can't find what you're looking for? Just reach out.

What information do I get per lead? ▾
By default: Full name, job title, company, verified email, and LinkedIn URL. Phone numbers available on Standard and Premium packages.
How do you verify emails? ▾
I use tools like Hunter.io and NeverBounce to verify every email before delivery. Target bounce rate is under 5%.
What format do I receive the leads in? ▾
Google Sheets or Excel — clean, formatted, and ready to import into HubSpot, Salesforce, or any outreach tool.
Can you target a specific niche or location? ▾
Yes — the more specific your targeting, the more accurate your list. I specialize in dental, healthcare, real estate, and SaaS industries.
How long does it take? ▾
100 leads: 24–48 hours. 300 leads: 3–5 days. 500+ leads or custom projects: 5–7 days. Rush delivery available.
Do you offer revisions or replacements? ▾
Yes — if any emails bounce above the 5% threshold, I replace them at no extra cost on Standard and Premium packages.
